On this day in history...

On this day in history, a pretty cool guy was born. His name was Darrin. God gave Darrin amazing gifts, such as gentleness, compassion, and discernment. Darrin grew up and attended Hogwarts to learn how to master his gifts even more. This experience changed his life. But then, suddenly God asked Darrin to move back to Evansville. At first, he didn't know why. But then, he met this pretty cool woman at the gym. It wasn't love at first sight, but slowly she grew on him. But then a massive pandemic hit-- they were separated! During their time apart, Darrin's girlfriend secretly learned how to code so she could surprise him with the birthday present he currently sees before him. So, here, my dear, is your 28th birthday present. Thanks for making life fun. I love you!
